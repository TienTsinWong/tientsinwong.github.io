Discrete Wavelet Transform on GPU

The algorithm is designed and developed by the graphics team (Jianqing Wang, Tien-Tsin Wong, Pheng-Ann Heng and Chi-Sing Leung) in The Chinese University of Hong Kong. We propose a fast GPU algorithm to perform the discrete wavelet transform featuring flexible boundary extension schemes, flexible wavelet kernels, Cg shader implementation,and high precision. One major beauty of our method is that both forward and inverse wavelet transforms are unified using our position-dependent filtering/convolution and the indirect addressing technique. The software is OPEN SOURCE and FREE for any commercial or academic usage. It is currently beta-released (version 0.9b) in two forms: (A) Jasper unofficial GPU extension, and (B) standalone DWTGPU C++ class module with demo program. Yes, we have successfully integrated the software into the well-known JPEG2000 software Jasper. All source codes and binaries can be downloaded at: http://www.cse.cuhk.edu.hk/~ttwong/software/dwtgpu/dwtgpu.html
